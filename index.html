TO MY DEAREST, HAMZA

To the most gentle man I have ever known, my favorite person, and the most thoughtful soul in my life — happiest birthday. You’ve always had this rare kind of thoughtfulness and understanding that makes people feel seen—something I’ve been lucky enough to share with you. You deserve every good thing this world has to offer, and while this website is just a small gesture, I hope it brings you even a fraction of the joy you’ve given me.

This is for you, and only for you. I’m so proud of all that you’ve accomplished, especially with your OGC group, and I’ll always be here, cheering for everything you will do next. You’ll always have a special place in my heart, and this website will stay here as long as that’s true.
Below are my undying flowers for you.
